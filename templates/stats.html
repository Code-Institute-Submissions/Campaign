As you can see the total number of donations were steadily rising over the years until a sudden drop in April. I aim to create a steadily increasing number of donations across the next year so that they can be correctly used by schools. The drastic difference in the number of donations for each state is tremendous. I guarantee to increasing funding in states with lower donations whilst keeping and not disturbing the high level in others.

Click on the other graphs, or even the drop down menu near the top of the page, to see the effect on the total number of donations, total number of donations and average donation for the state. The data on this page is from 20000 different donation with the average donation across the country being 405.

We can see from this that even though states like Texas have a low total number of donations, the average donations are over double that of states with a large number of total donations such as New York. I aim not only to increase the total number of donations in states like Texas, but also focus on improving the average donations in various states such as New York.
